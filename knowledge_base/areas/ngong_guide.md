# Ngong Area Guide

## Location Overview
Ngong is a scenic satellite town located approximately 25 kilometers southwest of Nairobi's CBD, nestled along the Ngong Hills. Known for its cool climate, spacious plots, and family-friendly environment, it has become increasingly popular among middle to upper-middle class buyers seeking a suburban lifestyle.

**Key Coordinates**: 25km from Nairobi CBD, Kajiado County
**Accessibility**: Ngong Road, Lang'ata Road

## Current Market Data (2025-2026)

### Price Ranges
- **Land/Plots**: KES 1,500,000 - KES 4,000,000 (⅛ acre to ½ acre)
- **2-Bedroom Apartments**: KES 3,500,000 - KES 5,500,000
- **3-Bedroom Townhouses**: KES 5,500,000 - KES 9,000,000
- **4-Bedroom Houses**: KES 9,000,000 - KES 18,000,000
- **½ Acre Plots**: KES 3,000,000 - KES 6,000,000 (premium for space)

### Appreciation Rate
**14.2% land price surge** (2025 data) - one of the top performers in Nairobi satellites. Driven by infrastructure improvements and lifestyle appeal.

### Rental Yields
- **2-Bedroom**: KES 25,000 - KES 35,000/month (7-9% annual yield)
- **3-Bedroom**: KES 35,000 - KES 55,000/month (7-9% annual yield)
- **4-Bedroom Houses**: KES 60,000 - KES 90,000/month (6-8% annual yield)

## Infrastructure & Connectivity

### Road Network
- **Ngong Road**: Main access route from Nairobi (under ongoing upgrades)
- **Lang'ata Road → Southern Bypass**: Alternative route (30-35 minutes to CBD)
- **Ngong-Suswa Road**: Connects to Karen, Kiserian
- **Internal Roads**: Mix of tarmac and murram (estate-dependent)
- **Rush Hour**: 45-60 minutes to CBD during peak

### Public Transport
- Matatus along Ngong Road (frequent services)
- Boda-bodas very common for last-mile connectivity
- Uber/Bolt available but limited in outer areas
- Transport cost to CBD: KES 100-150

### Utilities
- **Electricity**: Reliable KPLC connection
- **Water**: Mainly borehole + county water (some areas piped)
- **Internet**: 4G coverage, fiber expanding in estates
- **Sewerage**: Septic tanks common (few estates have sewerage)

## Climate & Environment - Major Selling Point

### Weather Advantages
- **Cool Climate**: 1,700-2,000m altitude = mild temperatures
- **Fresh Air**: Less pollution than Nairobi CBD
- **Greenery**: Trees, gardens, natural vegetation
- **Scenic Views**: Ngong Hills backdrop (iconic landmark)
- **Rainfall**: Good for gardens, agriculture

**Sales Angle**: "Escape Nairobi's heat and pollution - Ngong offers cool evenings and fresh air, perfect for kids playing outside and weekend family time."

2. **Southern Bypass Connection**: Reduced travel time to Karen, CBD
3. **Lifestyle Appeal**: Families seeking space, nature, tranquility
4. **Affordable Land**: Larger plots (½ acre) still accessible
5. **Self-Build Culture**: Many buy land, build custom homes

### Quality of Life Focus

- **Community**: Tight-knit neighborhoods, church groups
